Meet Serene Summer

Our Home on the Waves

"Serene Summer" isn't just a yacht; she is a true bluewater cruiser and a comfortable home designed for extended voyages. She's a magnificent Tayana 55, known for her robust build and exceptional seaworthiness, capable of handling diverse conditions with grace.

Equipped with all the modern conveniences—from a TV and coffee machine to an onboard bread maker—she truly combines the thrill of sailing with the comforts of home.

Staying Connected (Starlink)

Seamless Internet on the High Seas

In the vastness of the ocean, reliable internet can transform the cruising experience. We've embraced Starlink, and it's been a game-changer for staying in touch with loved ones, streaming shows, and accessing crucial weather data in even the most remote anchorages.

The benefits far outweigh the setup effort, offering us peace of mind and access to information wherever our voyages take us.

Our Nouméa Adventure

Highlights from New Caledonia

Our journey to Nouméa was a spectacular experience, filled with pristine islands and vibrant local culture. We explored the breathtaking Île des Pins, the "closest island to paradise," and the stunning lagoon of Île des Maîtres with its iconic overwater bungalows.

Back in the city, we immersed ourselves in the local culture at Nouméa's vibrant markets—a true feast for the senses that stocked our galley for delicious meals back on Serene Summer.

Magnetic Island Race Week

Thrill of the Tropics: Racing in Paradise

Magnetic Island Race Week is an annual highlight on the Australian sailing calendar, a fantastic blend of competitive racing and vibrant shoreside festivities in the stunning waters of North Queensland. We had an exhilarating time navigating the challenging courses, experiencing the adrenaline of close-quarters racing, and enjoying the camaraderie with fellow sailors.

From the strategic starts to the exhilarating finishes against the backdrop of Magnetic Island's beautiful bays, it’s an event that truly captures the spirit of tropical yacht racing. Evenings were spent celebrating with live music and delicious food, making it an unforgettable experience both on and off the water.
